Magic Cake

Magic cake is of the tastiest cakes the world has ever known. Few have tried it and lived to tell the tale, and these are their stories.

We also need to stress that whatever happens to you as a result of consuming Magic Cake is your responsibility. You have been warned, and you can view our legal page for more information

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pound of sugar
  • 3 sticks of butter

Directions

  1. Mix eggs, sugar, and butter in a large bowl.
  2. Spread onto a non-stick dish
  3. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour
  4. Let it sit at room temperature for 20 minutes
  5. Eat and Enjoy!
